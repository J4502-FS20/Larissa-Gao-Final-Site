All questions were based on information from CDC

1) Poor mental health increases the risk for chronic physical conditions like

Correct answer: All of the above.
Poor mental health increases the risk for heart diseases, stroke and cancer.

2) Is mental illnesses very common?

Correct answer: Yes.
About 1 in 5 people in the U.S. "will experience mental illness at some point in their lives," according to CDC.

3) Suicide is the ___ cause of death among people from 15 to 34 in the U.S.

Correct answer: 2nd.

4) Mental illnesses cannot be treated

Correct answer: No.

5) Half of mental illness occurs before a person turns __ years old

Correct answer: 14.

6) All people with mental illnesses are dangerous

Correct answer: No.
The majority of people with mental disorders are no more likely be violent than anyone else, according to CDC.

7) How many mental disorders defined by The Diagnostic and Statistical Manual Disorders?

Correct answer: Over 400.

Correct answer: None of them.
