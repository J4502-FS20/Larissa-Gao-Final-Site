Current treatment for mental illnesses

Most treatment methods for mental health disorders can be categorized as somatic treatments and psychotherapeutic treatments, according to Merck Manual Consumer Version. There are three kinds of therapies that psychiatrists normally use to treat patients.

1. Drug Therapy

A number of psychoative drugs are effective and highly used by psychiatrists and other medical doctors. These drugs are often categorized according to the mental disorder they are primarily prescribed for. For instance, antidepressants are used to treat depression.

2. Electroconvulsive therapy

Electrodes are placed on head for electroconvulsive therapy. A series of electrical shocks delivered to brain while the person is under anesthesia. The therapy has been shown to be the most effective for severe depression.

3. Psychotherapy

By creating an empathetic and accepting atmostphere, the therapist often is able to help the person identify the source of the problems and consider alterantives to deal with them. The therapy is appropriate and effective in a wide rage of conditions. Even people without mental disorders will find this method helpful.

Stress Relief

For people who don't have mental disorders, they need to learn how to relax to prevent themselves from having mental disorders. Here are some places to find professional suggestions on releasing pressure.

1. Headspace

The app is a guide to mindfulness for everyday life. Users can learn meditation and mindfulness skills from world-class experts to learn everything from managing stress to sleep.

2. Fitbit

Physical exercises can help release mental stress. Users can find free training videos on the app and also can subscribe for more videos.

3. Happify

The app has science-based activities and games that can help users overcome negative thought and release stress.
